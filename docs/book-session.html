Common Questions

How do payments work?expand_more

After your intro call, Angela will propose a bespoke coaching plan and send you a quote by email. Once agreed, a secure payment link is sent via PayPal or Revolut. Payment is required before sessions begin.

What is your cancellation policy?expand_more

Sessions can be cancelled or rescheduled free of charge up to 24 hours in advance. Late cancellations may incur a small processing fee.

What should I expect from the first session?expand_more

Your first paid 60-minute session builds on the intro call. We work through the plan together — identifying strengths, clarifying goals, and mapping out practical next steps.

Is my information private?expand_more

Absolutely. Confidentiality is the cornerstone of the coaching relationship. All sessions are private and conducted in a secure virtual environment.
