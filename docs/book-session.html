Common Questions

What happens after I submit the form?

Angela will be in touch within 24 hours to confirm your session, agree a time that suits you, and send a secure payment link if applicable.

Where do sessions take place?

All sessions are online via video call, so families anywhere in Ireland can access coaching without travel.

Is there a minimum number of sessions?

No. You can start with a single session or a free intro call. There's no obligation to commit to a package until you're confident it's right for you.

Can a parent attend a teen's session?

This is something Angela discusses with each family individually. Depending on the goals, sessions may be one-to-one with the teenager, or may include a parent — the format is always agreed in advance.

What if I need to reschedule?

Sessions can be rescheduled with at least 24 hours' notice, at no additional charge.
