What's Available

Coaching options built for your situation.

No two families are the same. These five options cover the most common starting points — and if yours is different, just get in touch.

Not sure where to start? Try a free 15-minute introductory call.

For teens

Teen Coaching

3–6 sessions · €255–€510

One-to-one sessions for teenagers aged 16–19. A confidential space to talk through pressure, explore strengths, and figure out what comes next — on their terms.

For teens who feel lost, overwhelmed, or simply unsure about the future.

For parents

Parent Support

3–6 sessions · €255–€510

Coaching for parents who want to understand their teenager better and show up without adding pressure. Practical guidance on how to listen, communicate, and let go of the things you can't control.

For parents who feel helpless or like they're always getting it wrong.

Next-Step Planning

4–8 sessions · €340–€680

A focused session (or series) dedicated to mapping out what comes after school. CAO choices, PLC options, apprenticeships, gap years — we look at everything and build a plan that actually makes sense.

For teens and parents who need clarity on post-secondary options.

For teens

Confidence & Motivation

3–6 sessions · €255–€510

For young people who have lost their drive — or never felt they had it. This coaching strand focuses on rebuilding self-belief from the ground up, using real strengths, not empty encouragement.

For teens who describe themselves as lazy, unmotivated, or not good enough.
